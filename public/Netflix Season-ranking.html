The Best Netflix Seasons According to Muhammad Asad

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Breaking Bed

Breaking Bad is the best because it masterfully depicts the moral descent of a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer, exploring themes of power, morality, and consequences with gripping intensity.

The Good Doctor

The Good Doctor stands out for its heartfelt portrayal of an autistic surgical resident, showcasing his exceptional medical skills while navigating personal and professional challenges with sincerity and empathy.

Stranger Things

Stranger Things captivates audiences with its nostalgic homage to 80s pop culture, compelling characters, and thrilling blend of supernatural mystery, friendship, and adventure in the small town of Hawkins
